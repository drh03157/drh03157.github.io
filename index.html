Tips and Tricks!

If you want truly special Ramen, follow these tips!


1) Add chopped onions or scallions after you cook your soup, to give a flavorful boost!

2) Add meat such as chicken or tuna to add more flavor and protein.

3) Only eat Ramen at most 10 times per week, in order to limit sodium intake!
